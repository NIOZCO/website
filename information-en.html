About me

I am a creative hobby artist and developer who enjoys working on drawings, worldbuilding, small programs and digital projects.

This website is my personal space to let my own ideas, worlds and projects gradually grow - without time pressure and purely for the joy of creating.

I work independently, on a small scale and without commercial intent. All content is created in my free time - as a long-term passion project.

About this website

This website was programmed by myself and is constantly being improved.

It serves as a central hub for my projects - including:

  • drawings and illustrations

  • my own fantasy world

  • small software and web projects

  • personal creative experiments

New content will appear gradually - as soon as it is ready.

Transparency, sources and licenses

It is important to me to work openly and transparently.

Therefore, the following applies to all content on this website:

  • Sources are always indicated when I use third-party information

  • Licenses are correctly stated when using third-party material

  • My own works are clearly marked

  • AI-generated content is clearly labeled (if applicable)

If content appears on external platforms, their guidelines also apply.

Note on AI usage

In a time when AI can generate texts, images and music at the push of a button, I consciously choose a different path.

I only use artificial intelligence as support - never as a replacement for creative work.

My projects are created mainly through my own work:

  • drawings - traditional or digital

  • self-programmed tools and websites

  • my own designs and ideas

  • image editing without AI filters

AI may occasionally assist, for example:

  • when thinking about alternatives

  • for brainstorming

  • for technical support

However, the creative result always comes from me - or is created in human collaboration.

If AI was involved in content creation, this will be clearly indicated.

Why hardly any AI?

AI does not create content from nothing - it is based on existing works, data and ideas. Because of this, many AI-generated results are legally and ethically difficult to classify and are often not clearly “original works”.

I therefore prefer to create my content myself:

  • personally

  • creatively

  • with traceable protection

This way, my projects retain their own unique character.
