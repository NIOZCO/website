My own fantasy world

This page is dedicated to a completely original fantasy world that I am developing step by step. It includes places, cultures, creatures, plants, objects, stories and much more. Everything you see here is created by me and will slowly grow over time.

This world is a long-term project:

In the future, it may become the foundation for books, games, illustrations, films, series or other creative works. Right now, it is still in development - expanding with every new idea.

What you will find here

On this page you’ll find a structured collection of content connected to this fantasy world, such as:

  • Plants and magical flora

  • Creatures and beings

  • Cities, lands and landscapes

  • Cultures and societies

  • Objects, artifacts and weapons

  • Myths, legends and historical events

  • Diary entries, notes and in-world documents

Each entry will be added gradually — sometimes with drawings, sometimes as text, sometimes both.

Why I am creating this world

I have always wanted to build my own world — a place full of imagination, mystery and stories.

For me, this project is:

  • a creative journey

  • a playground for ideas

  • and a personal form of artistic expression

There is no rush. I work on this world slowly and calmly — and only publish things when I am happy with them.

Note

This fantasy world currently does not yet use its official title publicly. That may change in the future - until then, all content is presented under my artist name NIOZCO.
